Project

3D Futuristic Motorcycle – Design and Rendering: This project involved the design and modeling of a futuristic motorcycle with a stylized and conceptual approach. The goal was to combine aerodynamic shapes, neon lighting, and modern details that evoke speed, advanced technology, and an attractive visual style, ideal for futuristic video games or cinematics.

Creative and Technical Process:

  • Concept and Design: I started with a basic idea inspired by sci-fi motorcycles, adding unique visual elements like the tron-like light-up wheels and a wraparound silhouette that conveys dynamism. I incorporated custom details like decals and fictional logos that add character and storytelling to the model.
  • 3D Modeling: I used hard surface modeling techniques to accurately define each section of the bike, from the frame to the finest details of the body. The modeling was done in Maya.
  • Texturing and Materials: I applied materials with metallic reflections, chrome accents, and shiny surfaces. Textures were carefully created and adjusted to highlight the volumes and bring the surfaces to life under different lighting conditions.
  • Lighting and Rendering: I used an environment with intense RGB lights and neon effects to reinforce the futuristic look. The final render was done in Maya, with special attention to composition, reflections, and colors to achieve a striking and professional image.

The result is a motorcycle with a strong visual identity, designed to stand out in action scenes, movies, or cyberpunk video games. This project allowed me to explore aggressive shapes, advanced lighting effects, and visual storytelling within 3D vehicle design.
